# Regulatory Research Prompt

1. What is your **product idea**?
2. Which **country or region** will it launch in?

If there is already a product idea in this chat, proceed ahead.

Once you provide these, follow this 5-step process below:

---

Act as a **Regulatory Analyst** for a Product Manager.

Help me research and summarise all regulatory considerations for my product idea based on the information provided above.

---

### Step 1: Identify Relevant Regulations

- List all laws, policies, and governing bodies that apply to this industry and product type in the specified country.
- Include:
    - Industry-specific regulations (e.g., transport, finance, data, health)
    - Consumer protection and liability rules
    - Employment / contractor laws (if applicable)
    - Data privacy and security requirements
    - Advertising, pricing, or marketplace regulations
- Provide links or names of official authorities (e.g., FTC, FDA, ASIC, GDPR).

---

### Step 2: Conduct Regulatory Research

- Summarise the key compliance requirements for each relevant regulation.
- Note any regional differences (federal vs state or provincial laws).
- Highlight areas that require registration, licensing, or certification.

---

### Step 3: Assess Product Compliance

- Evaluate how the current product concept aligns or conflicts with these regulations.
- Identify potential compliance gaps, risks, or grey areas.
- Classify risks as **low / medium / high** for each major category.

---

### Step 4: Develop Compliance Strategies

- Suggest concrete strategies to achieve compliance or reduce regulatory risk.
- Include:
    - Required documentation or disclosures
    - Recommended internal policies or audits
    - Partnerships or certifications that could simplify compliance.

---

### Step 5: Monitor and Update

- Recommend how to stay updated (e.g., newsletters, government databases, regulatory alerts).
- Suggest how often to review or reassess compliance (e.g., quarterly, annually).

---

### Summary: Regulatory Risk Outlook

End with a summary paragraph titled **"Regulatory Risk Outlook"** that explains:

- Whether this is a **low, medium, or high** regulatory-risk environment for the product.
- Rate the **regulatory risk out of 10** (1 = minimal, 10 = severe).
- Include 1â€“2 sentences on what the Product Manager should prioritise before launch.

---

Format the answer in clear sections with concise, bullet-pointed insights.
